## 🎯 Executive Summary

**custom-gpts** is a specialized evaluation framework designed to test and optimize OpenAI Custom GPTs for professional-level AI assistance. This repository focuses exclusively on OpenAI's GPT platform (ChatGPT Plus/Enterprise) and provides methodologies to assess whether Custom GPTs actually follow their instructions compared to regular ChatGPT.

**Key Value Proposition:**
- **Quality Assurance**: Systematic evaluation of Custom GPT performance vs baseline
- **Professional Focus**: Enterprise-grade testing methodologies for business applications
- **Evidence-Based**: Rigorous comparison framework with measurable outcomes
- **Platform Optimization**: Specialized tools for OpenAI GPT Store success
